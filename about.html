Hey, I'm Dezso

I was born in 1973 in Hungary. I finished schools here and then I got a jobs here as well.

Later, in 2006, I moved to Ireland. I started here to take photography seriously, and then I have taken several courses succesfully. I am still training myself to this day because you can always learn something new.

I'm an adventure, landscapes, lifestyle, aerial and portrait photographer based in north Co Dublin.

Luckily I visited many countries, I discovered a lot of beautiful places, I met wonderful people, interesting cultures.
